How Verification Works
  1. The verifier receives an email with a unique link
  2. They review your evidence without needing to create an account
  3. They confirm, qualify, or decline each claim
  4. Their response is recorded with their name and role
  5. The link expires after 14 days
Tips
  • Choose people who directly observed your work
  • Managers and team leads provide the strongest verification
  • Use their work email for credibility
  • You can send up to 20 verification requests per day
