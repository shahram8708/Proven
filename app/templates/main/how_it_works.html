How Proven Works

From invisible skills to verified evidence — in three steps

Step 1

Submit Your Evidence

Document real work using our structured evidence framework. We guided you to detail:

  • Project Context — What was the situation and your role?
  • Challenge Faced — What problem needed solving?
  • Approach Taken — What was your strategy and execution?
  • Key Decisions — What trade-offs did you navigate?
  • Measurable Outcomes — What results did you achieve?
  • Lessons Learned — What would you do differently?

Our AI automatically extracts skills from your evidence and scores its quality across multiple dimensions.

Step 2

Get Verified

Send verification requests to people who witnessed your work — managers, teammates, clients, or collaborators.

  • Token-based access — Verifiers don't need a Proven account
  • Specific responses — They confirm, qualify, or decline each claim
  • Identity tracked — Name, role, and relationship recorded
  • 14-day window — Links expire to prevent stale requests

Each verification raises your profile's trust score, making you more visible to employers.

Step 3

Get Discovered

Your verified evidence profile becomes searchable by employers looking for real, proven talent.

  • Skill-based search — Employers find you by demonstrated abilities
  • Verification filter — Higher verification rates = more visibility
  • Quality ranking — Evidence quality score influences your ranking
  • Domain matching — Appear in searches relevant to your expertise

No applications needed. Employers come to you because your work speaks for itself.
